id: oversight-leadership-implication
title: 'The leadership implication is uncomfortable but unavoidable'
---

Teams that insist engineers must read and fully understand every AI-generated line will face one of two outcomes: velocity collapses, or the rule is quietly ignored.

Teams that teach oversight as a skill—planning, constraint design, validation, and recovery—will scale AI responsibly without pretending manual certainty still exists. The industry is already making this shift. The question is whether teams will acknowledge it deliberately or stumble into it implicitly.
