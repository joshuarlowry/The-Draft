The shift is not just about reducing labor cost. It is about redesigning work around where AI is already being used. Anthropic reports usage is concentrated in software and technical writing tasks, with uneven penetration by occupation.[[cite:anthropic_usage_concentration_quote]]

That matters because present adoption is still mixed-mode: more augmentation than full replacement.[[cite:anthropic_augmentation_57_quote]] Teams therefore need explicit role architecture for who delegates, who verifies, and who escalates.

This is one reason static pyramid models are under pressure. PwC describes a “diamond-shaped structure” with a smaller base and stronger middle layer, which implies capability-depth decisions are replacing simple headcount growth assumptions.[[cite:pwc_no_more_pyramids_generalist_quote]]
