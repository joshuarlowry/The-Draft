Design isn't disappearing under AI; it's being clarified. Teams that defined design mostly as producing screens will see that activity automated quickly, but that was always only one layer of the discipline.

The enduring layer is upstream: understanding context, exploring constraints, and choosing a viable path through ambiguity. That's the part that governs whether faster output creates value or just more noise. As Arango puts it, "[[cite:arango_abductive_reasoning_here_to_stay_quote]]"

For IA and UX leaders, the implication is straightforward: measure design teams on decision quality and problem framing, not just deliverable throughput.
