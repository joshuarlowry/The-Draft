Current usage patterns suggest humans are not disappearing from work; they are moving up the abstraction stack.[[cite:anthropic_augmentation_57_quote]]

That shift increases the importance of organizational capability design. DORA’s framing is useful here: AI amplifies existing organizational strengths and weaknesses rather than bypassing them.[[cite:dora_ai_amplifier_quote]]

In practice, this means teams cannot assume skills will mature automatically. As implementation moves toward AI and humans provide direction, companies need explicit progression paths from supervised prompt-and-review tasks toward architecture, quality ownership, and decision accountability.[[cite:sourcetms_soft_skills_direction_quote]]
